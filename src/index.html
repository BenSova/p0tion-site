About p0tion

p0tion is the first ever semi-untethered jailbreak working on all devices through iOS 12 - 14.3! It comes with Sileo for a more modern jailbreak feel and will kick off iOS 14's jailbreaking era!

Supported iOS devices and versions

iOS versions 12.0 to 14.3 are currently supported, but only arm64e devices are compatible. This means you will only be able to jailbreak your iPhone XR or newer. Support for other devices will come at a later date.
